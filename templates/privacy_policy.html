🔐 Privacy Policy - Qadam

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

Qadam is a habit-building application designed to help users develop positive routines before accessing selected social media applications.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Qadam does not collect or store personal data on external servers.

The app may process the following information locally on your device:

  • Selected app usage status (to determine when a blocked app is opened)
  • Task completion status
  • Streak and progress statistics

All data is stored locally on the user's device unless explicitly stated otherwise.

2. Accessibility Service Usage

Qadam uses Android Accessibility Service permission solely for the following purposes:

  • Detecting when selected social media applications are opened
  • Displaying a task completion screen before allowing access

Qadam does NOT:

  • Record screen content
  • Collect typed text
  • Capture personal messages
  • Share accessibility data with third parties

Accessibility data is processed locally and is never transmitted to external servers.

3. Data Sharing

Qadam does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

4. Data Security

All user data is stored locally on the device.

We take reasonable measures to ensure data protection.

5. Children's Privacy

Qadam is not specifically designed for children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Users are advised to review this page periodically.
